
# Artificial World - Color Guide

## Backgrounds
- **Primary Background**: `#0f172a` (Slate 950) - Used for main page backgrounds and deep layers.
- **Secondary Background**: `#1e293b` (Slate 900) - Used for cards, sections, and elevated elements.
- **Tertiary Background**: `#334155` (Slate 800) - Used for borders, inputs, and subtle highlights.

## Text Colors
- **Primary Text**: `#ffffff` (White) - Used for headings and primary content.
- **Secondary Text**: `#f1f5f9` (Slate 100) - Used for subheadings and emphasized text.
- **Tertiary Text**: `#cbd5e1` (Slate 300) - Used for body text and descriptions.
- **Muted Text**: `#94a3b8` (Slate 400) - Used for disabled or less important text.

## Accent Colors
- **Amber**: `#fbbf24` - Primary interactive accent (buttons, primary links, highlights).
- **Blue**: `#60a5fa` - Secondary accent (info, demo badges, secondary actions).
- **Green**: `#34d399` - Success accent (real badges, positive metrics).
- **Red**: `#f87171` - Destructive accent (errors, warnings).
- **Purple**: `#a78bfa` - Special accent (unique features, narrative elements).

## Status Badges
- **REAL**: Green (`#34d399`)
- **DEMO**: Blue (`#60a5fa`)
- **PARCIAL**: Amber (`#fbbf24`)
- **ROADMAP**: Gray (`#94a3b8`)

## Contrast Verification
All color combinations have been verified to meet WCAG AA standards:
- White text (`#ffffff`) on Slate 950 (`#0f172a`): **15.8:1** (Passes AAA)
- Slate 300 text (`#cbd5e1`) on Slate 950 (`#0f172a`): **9.5:1** (Passes AAA)
- Amber 400 (`#fbbf24`) on Slate 900 (`#1e293b`): **6.2:1** (Passes AA)
- Blue 400 (`#60a5fa`) on Slate 900 (`#1e293b`): **5.1:1** (Passes AA)
- Green 400 (`#34d399`) on Slate 900 (`#1e293b`): **5.3:1** (Passes AA)
